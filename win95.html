About Windows 95

Windows 95 was released to manufacturing on July 14, 1995, and became generally available on August 24, 1995.
It was a significant upgrade from Windows 3.1, featuring a new graphical user interface, improved "plug-and-play" functionality, and support for 32-bit applications and longer file names.
The launch was a major event, with midnight release parties and long lines at stores, fueled by a massive $300 million marketing campaign.
